Fun with MathML

Einstein's famous equation:

E 2 = m ⁢ c 2

Why wasn’t MathML widely used until recently?

Despite being in the HTML specification for a long time, MathML adoption was limited due to:

Browser Support: Many popular browsers, like Google Chrome, did not support MathML natively until recently. Without cross-browser compatibility, developers had to rely on workarounds like MathJax or other libraries.

Performance Issues: Early implementations of MathML in browsers were often slow, making it impractical for complex mathematical content.

With the recent improvements in browser compatibility, including support in modern versions of Chrome and Edge, MathML is becoming more practical for developers.
